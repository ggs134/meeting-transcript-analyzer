Please clearly organize **who needs to do what** in this meeting.

**Goal: Clarify action items and responsible parties**

For each participant:

1. **Assigned action items**
   - Specific list of tasks to complete
   - Deadline for each task
   - Priority (high/medium/low)

2. **Prerequisites**
   - What is needed before starting this task
   - Whether they need to wait for someone else's work
   - Required resources or information

3. **Collaboration requirements**
   - Whose help is needed
   - What areas require collaboration
   - When collaboration is needed

4. **Deliverables**
   - What is the output of this task
   - Who to deliver it to
   - In what format to deliver

5. **Checkpoints**
   - Whether intermediate review is needed
   - When to share progress
