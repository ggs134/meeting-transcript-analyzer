Based on the meeting transcript, extract and organize the following for each participant:

**The goal is to organize what each participant did and what they will do.**

1. Ideas (Ideation):
   - What new ideas or proposals did they suggest?
   - What problems did they identify?
   - What solutions did they propose?

2. Work coordination (Work coordination):
   - Scope: What work scope or responsibilities did they define?
   - Schedule: What schedules or deadlines did they coordinate?

3. Work reporting (Work reporting):
   - Completed work: What work did they report as completed since the last meeting?
   - Planned work: What work did they say they would do going forward?
   - Progress: What is the current status of their work?

4. Quantitative contribution (Quantitative contribution):
   - Number of statements: How many times did they speak?
   - Word count: Approximately how many words did they speak?
   - Speaking proportion: What percentage of the total conversation did they contribute?

**Response format:**
Organize by participant according to the above items.

**Overall summary:**
- Main topics discussed in this meeting
- Key decisions made
- Next steps

**Important Note:**
- When mentioning deadlines or dates, **calculate the specific date (YYYY-MM-DD) based on the meeting date**.
- Do NOT use relative terms like 'next week', 'tomorrow', or 'this weekend'.
