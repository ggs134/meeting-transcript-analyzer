Please organize the **knowledge, information, and insights** shared in this meeting.

**Goal: Make knowledge from the meeting easy to reference later**

For each participant:

1. **Technical knowledge**
   - What technical information did they share?
   - What methods or approaches did they introduce?
   - What tools or technologies did they mention?

2. **Domain knowledge**
   - What industry or domain knowledge did they share?
   - What trends or changes did they mention?
   - What best practices did they introduce?

3. **Lessons learned**
   - What lessons did they share from experience?
   - What mistakes did they warn about?
   - What tips did they provide?

4. **Resources**
   - What documents or links did they share?
   - What references did they recommend?
   - What contacts did they introduce?

**Key takeaways:**
- What are the most important things to remember from this meeting?
- What should be applied immediately?
- What should be studied further?

**Important Note:**
- When mentioning deadlines or dates, **calculate the specific date (YYYY-MM-DD) based on the meeting date**.
- Do NOT use relative terms like 'next week', 'tomorrow', or 'this weekend'.
