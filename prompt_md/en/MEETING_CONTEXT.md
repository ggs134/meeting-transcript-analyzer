Please understand the **context and flow** of this meeting.

**Goal: Understand how the meeting progressed**

For each participant:

1. **Flow within the meeting**
   - What did they do at the beginning of the meeting?
   - What about the middle part?
   - What about the latter part?

2. **Contribution to discussion flow**
   - What topics did they initiate?
   - What topics did they actively participate in?
   - What topics did they conclude?

**Overall meeting progression:**
- How did the meeting start?
- How did it develop?
- How did it conclude?

**Notable points:**
- What were the turning points in the meeting?
- What were the most important moments?
- What topics generated the most discussion?

**Important Note:**
- When mentioning deadlines or dates, **calculate the specific date (YYYY-MM-DD) based on the meeting date**.
- Do NOT use relative terms like 'next week', 'tomorrow', or 'this weekend'.
