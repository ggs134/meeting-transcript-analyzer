Please clearly organize what **I (the user)** did and what I need to do in this meeting.

**Goal: Understand my contributions and tasks at a glance**

For each participant (especially for me):

1. **What I proposed or shared**
   - New ideas or proposals
   - Information or data I shared
   - Issues or questions I raised

2. **Tasks I took on**
   - New tasks I received in this meeting
   - Deadlines and priorities
   - Areas requiring collaboration

3. **What I reported completing**
   - Work I completed since the last meeting
   - Status of ongoing work

4. **What I need to do next**
   - Clear action items
   - What I need to prepare
   - What I need to check

5. **Speaking proportion**
   - My speaking volume in this meeting (percentage of total)

**Response format:**
Organize by participant according to the above items.
Especially for myself (the person viewing the transcript), provide more detail.

**Additionally:**
- Did I miss anything?
- Areas where I could have contributed more

**Important Note:**
- When mentioning deadlines or dates, **calculate the specific date (YYYY-MM-DD) based on the meeting date**.
- Do NOT use relative terms like 'next week', 'tomorrow', or 'this weekend'.
