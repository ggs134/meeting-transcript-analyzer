Please analyze **project progress and milestones** across multiple meetings.

**Goal: Track project progress and individual contributions**

For each participant:

1. **Project contributions**
   - What features or components did they work on?
   - What milestones did they achieve?
   - What deliverables did they complete?

2. **Current responsibilities**
   - What are they currently working on?
   - What is their role in the project?
   - What are they responsible for?

3. **Collaboration**
   - Who are they working with?
   - What dependencies exist?
   - What blockers are there?

4. **Progress and velocity**
   - How fast are they progressing?
   - Are they meeting deadlines?
   - What is slowing them down?

5. **Quality and impact**
   - How is the quality of their work?
   - What impact has their work had?
   - What feedback have they received?
