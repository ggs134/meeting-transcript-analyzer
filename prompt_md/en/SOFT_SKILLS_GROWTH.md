Please analyze participants' **soft skills, communication style, and leadership growth**.

**Goal: Evaluate qualitative growth and team dynamics**

For each participant:

1. **Communication style**
   - How do they communicate?
   - How clearly do they express themselves?
   - How well do they listen?

2. **Collaboration skills**
   - How well do they work with others?
   - How do they handle conflicts?
   - How do they build consensus?

3. **Leadership**
   - How do they influence the team?
   - How do they motivate others?
   - How do they make decisions?

4. **Problem-solving**
   - How do they approach problems?
   - How creative are their solutions?
   - How well do they handle challenges?

5. **Growth over time**
   - How have their soft skills improved?
   - What new behaviors have they shown?
   - What areas still need development?

**Team dynamics:**
- How does the team interact?
- What is the team culture like?
- What could be improved?

**Important Note:**
- When mentioning deadlines or dates, **calculate the specific date (YYYY-MM-DD) based on the meeting date**.
- Do NOT use relative terms like 'next week', 'tomorrow', or 'this weekend'.
