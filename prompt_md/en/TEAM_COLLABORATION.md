Please understand **what role each team member plays** and **who they need to collaborate with** in this meeting.

**Goal: Understand team member roles and collaboration structure**

For each participant:

1. **Their current role**
   - Main work areas covered in this meeting
   - Key responsibilities they manage
   - Areas where they showed expertise

2. **What they provided**
   - Help they gave to other team members
   - Information or resources they shared
   - Solutions they proposed

3. **What they need**
   - What they requested from other team members
   - Areas where they need help
   - Tasks requiring collaboration

4. **Collaboration relationships**
   - Who they are working with
   - What areas they are collaborating on
   - Points to note when collaborating

5. **When to contact them**
   - What topics to contact them about
   - What to ask them

**Response format:**
[Participant name]
- Current role:
- What they provided:
- What they need:
- Collaboration relationships:
- Contact points:

**Overall team structure:**
- Summary of main collaboration lines
- Understanding information flow

**Important Note:**
- When mentioning deadlines or dates, **calculate the specific date (YYYY-MM-DD) based on the meeting date**.
- Do NOT use relative terms like 'next week', 'tomorrow', or 'this weekend'.
